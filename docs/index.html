Who is Alex the Jester?


This gibberish-speaking jester is the brainchild of Alex Feldman, who has devoted his life to connecting with people around the world, bringing them laughter and insight. An accidental expert on nonverbal communication, Alex is a master of using face, body, and tone of voice. In his performances, speaking engagements, andTED Talk, Alex, shows how anyone can be eloquent beyond words—and have better relationships at work and in life.

ABOUT ALEX THE JESTER

Laugh ‘til you cry with Alexander, King of Jesters. He’s dazzled audiences on Showtime, America’s Got Talent, at Just for Laughs in Montreal, and off-Broadway in New York.
